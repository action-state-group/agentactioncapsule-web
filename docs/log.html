The public log

The append-only transparency log, read live from the public anchor: total entries, the latest signed checkpoint, how long it's been running, and the most recent records. It's early and small — that's the point of showing it honestly: it's public, so anyone can read it and verify any entry, and it only grows. Records carry a payload hash, never your data.
